Mentes en Acción

Usability Evaluation for Older Adults.

This project is a usability assessment of a mobile app prototype called "Mentes en acción", using the think-aloud protocol. We developed a protocol for a 15-task test aimed at adults over 65 years old, in order to gather insights about their user experience. Based on the findings, we conducted an analysis and provided suggestions to address the identified issues.

LogiCubes

Heuristic and Usability Evaluation of an Educational Video Game.

Logicubes: The Path is a work-in-progress video game developed by the Colombian studio Indie Level Studio. It is a puzzle game where players progress using commands that build skills necessary for programming.
We examined various aspects that influence player interaction and overall experience with the game. A heuristic evaluation was applied during user testing to assess the game's usability and satisfaction. Based on the collected data, we analyzed the findings and suggested improvements to address the issues identified during the evaluation.

BRANDING

Talking with Alejandra, I discovered in her a passion for nature and, even more so, for naturalness, both in the environment and in the body. I sought to address the issue of identity by defining three values that represented both her work and her personality: versatility, impact, and organicity. From her broad portfolio of photographs, I selected those that evoked these values. The color palette and typography were defined based on a series of her works where blue and yellow tones were abundant, alongside organic and fluid forms.

Lastly, the repeated use of the body as a graphic identity element comes from its ability to embody all three concepts: versatility, in terms of the many metaphors and references we attribute to it; organicity, due to the naturalness of its lines and shapes, more arbitrary than calculated; and finally, the impact it generates through its expressiveness.

The visual identity of Exagon is based on the concept of duality between industry and ecology, present in the company's approach. This concept is explicitly expressed both in the combination of shapes and colors chosen for the identity, and in the structure of the logo and the color selection.

The use of the hexagon is central to the construction of the symbol and the complementary graphics, and as an additional resource, the pattern generated by the arrangement of hexagons is incorporated, referencing the beehive— a key species in the production process of the company's flagship product.

Ari Bakery is an artisanal bakery founded in 2022, specializing in the creation of delicious and carefully presented products, made with high-quality ingredients and traditional techniques.

Ari Bakery's logo was designed using its name and initials, taking inspiration from cursive or handwriting styles. One of the main inspirations was butterflies—specifically their profile shape, which was used to design the uppercase 'B' in the logo. Additionally, the stem of the 'B' also functions as the stem of the lowercase 'a'.
